What is GeoSnap?

GeoSnap is a geotagging photography app designed with landscape photographers in mind.

Designed for photographers

GeoSnap is designed to help you keep track of exactly where you took your photos, making it easier to geotag the exact location where you took your shot!

Time saver

With GeoSnap, long gone are the days of endlessly poring over Google Maps to find your exact geolocation after the fact.

Helpful

GeoSnap has a built in weather app to help you plan out your next trip. Never wonder whether or not you should bring a jacket again!

Travel-friendly

GeoSnap is designed with hiking and travel in mind. We make sure our app is optimized to use as little of your battery as possible.

Your Devices Stay Synced

All your tags and settings stay synced between all your devices. Never worry about mismatched settings and data again.

Less files included

We at GeoSnap understand that storage space is at a premium. We make sure to keep our app as small as possible so that you have more room for the rest of your apps and data.

Features

  • Works Offline
  • Uses less battery
  • Helps you stay organized
  • Stays synced with the cloud
  • Accurate to the smallest movement
  • Always 100% free of charge
  • Compatible with iOS5 and newer devices
